## 🚀 Overview

**Mario_Game** is a classic 2D platformer game inspired by the iconic Super Mario series.  
Join Mario on his adventure to rescue Princess Peach by overcoming enemies, jumping over obstacles, and collecting coins!

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Classic Gameplay** — Run, jump, and stomp enemies.
- 🌎 **Multiple Levels** — Explore diverse and challenging stages.
- 🍄 **Power-ups** — Collect mushrooms, fire flowers, and stars for special abilities.
- 💰 **Score System** — Gather coins and boost your score.
- 🎮 **Responsive Controls** — Supports keyboard and touch input.
- 🔊 **Sound Effects & Music** — Retro-style audio for immersive gameplay.
- 📱 **Mobile Friendly** — Play on desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
- 🏆 **Leaderboard (Coming Soon)** — Compete for high scores!
